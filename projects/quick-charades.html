About

     is a small game for Android, where you pit yourself against another to see who can guess a series of pictures the fastest. The idea was thought up by Josue Segurra, our lead designer. This was made four a Java course at PCC (CS 3B).

Usage

    Currently, is simply just a prototype. You play against the clock instead of against another, and you are given a randomized series of pictures (there is only a small number of pictures). You must guess what each picture is before the time runs out. You can choose to skip a picture, if you are having trouble.

Development

Overview

     was made in Java, using Android Studio. We also used Genymotion to simulate an Android device to aid in testing. The game simply randomizes a list of images and their names, randomly adds letters to the letter set, randomizes it, and presents it to the player. It then sets the timer and has it count down. Once the player reaches the end or the timer runs out, the game calculates the player's scores.

Design Document

Future

     is currently a prototype. When we were creating this game, we spent most of our time learning Android development, database management, and networking. As such, there are many features that we had hoped to implement, but are currently missing. This includes:

  • Account management: Create an account to add friends and save progress.
  • Head to head challenges: Play against other people; whoever scores the best wins.
  • Leaderboards: Your scores will be saved and ranked in a leaderboard with other people and your friends/
  • Friends: Add friends, and challenge them.
  • Categories: Different categories have different sets of images.
  • Image obscuration: The images presented are obscured and stylized in some way.
  • Power ups: Collect power ups for doing well in games, and use them to help you in a game or hinder your opponent.
  • Endless mode: Keep guessing images until you make the incorrect choice.
